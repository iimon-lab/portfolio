Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how iimon_lab_posting (the "App"), operated by iimon_lab, handles information when you use the App to automate posting to your own Instagram and Facebook accounts via the Meta API.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects and processes only the minimum information necessary to function:

  • Basic profile information of your Instagram Business or Creator account (such as account ID and username), solely for identifying the target account for posting.
  • Access tokens issued by Meta, used to authenticate and publish content on your behalf.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively for the following purpose:

  • To automate the publishing of content to the account owner's own Instagram and/or Facebook accounts.

We do not use your data for analytics, advertising, profiling, or any other purpose beyond the posting functionality described above.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your data to any third parties. The App only accesses the account owner's own data and does not access or process any third-party user data.

5. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens are stored exclusively in a local environment file (.env) on the account owner's own machine. No data is transmitted to or stored on any external server, cloud service, or database. The App runs entirely in a local environment.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Access tokens are retained locally only for as long as the App is in use. You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Removing the local .env file containing the access token.
  • Revoking the App's access permissions through your Meta Account Settings (Settings > Business Integrations).

Once access is revoked, the App will no longer be able to interact with your account.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
